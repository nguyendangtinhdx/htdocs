About

We are a small group interested in Dublin's cultural history and modernisation projects.

This website was established with the aim of drawing attention to the Boland's Mill site, in light of its industrial and historical significance to the Dublin area.

If you would like more information, you can contact us via the Boland's Mill Facebook page or on Twitter.
